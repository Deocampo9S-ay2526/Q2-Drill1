EMERGENCY GO-BAG!!!

Emergency Food and Water

Keep 1L of water and food that doesn't go bad easily! Stay hydrated and don't starve.

First Aid

Include medical supplies you'd naturally find in a first aid kit. Ex: Bandaids, anti-bacterial solutions, pain relievers, etc.

Flashlights and Whistles

Pack a flashlight in order to see when it's dark and a whistle in case of an emergency in order to locate a person or yourself with rescuers.

Clothes

Bring clothes in case of wet or damaged garments to prevent causing further sickness and to stay warm..
